# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: April 14, 2026**

Welcome to **Unfoundry** ([https://unfoundry.co.uk](https://unfoundry.co.uk)). Your privacy is a fundamental priority for us. Because we believe in a transparent and open web, our approach to privacy is simple: **we do not track you.**

### 1\. No Tracking and No Cookies

The Unfoundry website is designed to be a "zero-tracking" environment.

  * **No Cookies:** We do not use cookies, local storage, or any similar technologies to identify you or track your movement across the web.
  * **No Analytics Requests:** We do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Analytics) that inject tracking scripts or collect IP addresses.
  * **No Server Logs:** Our hosting infrastructure is configured to minimize data retention. We do not aggregate personal data from your visit to our site.

### 2\. How We Gauge Interest

Rather than using invasive tracking to measure our reach, Unfoundry gauges interest and project success through community interaction on our official GitHub organization.

  * **GitHub Interactions:** We monitor public metrics such as "Stars," "Forks," "Issues," and "Pull Requests" on our repositories.
  * **Third-Party Privacy:** When you interact with us on GitHub, your data is subject to the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

### 3\. Open Source Libraries and CDN Tracking

Unfoundry utilizes various open-source libraries to power our software products.

  * **External Assets:** Some of these libraries may rely on Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) for delivery. These CDNs may perform their own form of basic request tracking (such as logging IP addresses for security or traffic analysis).
  * **User Control:** Because our products are open-source, you have full visibility. You can easily monitor the network requests originating from our products.
  * **Customization:** Advanced users are encouraged to audit these requests. If you wish to avoid CDN interaction, the open-source nature of our code allows you to tweak these libraries to be served locally or to swap them for alternate sources that meet your specific privacy requirements.

### 4\. Supporting Software Products

Unfoundry develops libraries and utilities intended for integration into other software products (such as game mods or developer tools).

  * **Library Autonomy:** While Unfoundry libraries themselves do not contain tracking or telemetry code, the "parent" software products that utilize our libraries may have their own data collection practices.
  * **Independent Documentation:** Please refer to the privacy policies of the specific software products or platforms where Unfoundry libraries are implemented.

### 5\. Transparency and Source Code

We believe that the best way to prove privacy is through transparency.

  * **Auditability:** All source code for Unfoundry products is publicly available on our [Official GitHub Repository](https://www.google.com/url?sa=E&source=self&q=https://github.com/Unfoundry). We encourage users to inspect our code to verify our privacy and security claims.
  * **Permissive Licensing:** Our projects are typically released under permissive licenses, ensuring you have the right to view, modify, and audit the software you use.

### 6\. External Links

Our website may contain links to other websites (such as GitHub or Discord). Once you leave our domain, we are no longer responsible for the privacy practices of those external sites.

### 7\. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our commitment to your privacy, please contact us via our GitHub organization.
